REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
Software Development Services

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
The client seeks proposals for the development of a comprehensive web-based application for tender management and analysis.

2. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
2.1 Company Registration
- Valid company registration certificate
- Minimum 3 years of operation
- Good standing with regulatory authorities

2.2 Financial Requirements
- Financial statements for the last 3 years
- Minimum annual turnover of $500,000
- Positive cash flow for the last 2 years

2.3 Technical Requirements
- ISO 9001 certification
- Minimum 5 years of experience in software development
- At least 3 similar projects completed successfully
- Team of minimum 5 developers with relevant experience

2.4 Legal Requirements
- Valid business license
- Tax compliance certificate
- No pending legal disputes

3. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
3.1 Technology Stack
- Frontend: React.js or Angular
- Backend: Node.js or Python
- Database: PostgreSQL or MongoDB
- Cloud hosting capability

3.2 Functional Requirements
- User authentication and authorization
- Document upload and management
- AI-powered analysis capabilities
- Reporting and analytics
- Mobile responsive design

4. PROJECT TIMELINE
- Project duration: 6 months
- Phase 1: Requirements analysis and design (1 month)
- Phase 2: Development (4 months)
- Phase 3: Testing and deployment (1 month)

5. EVALUATION CRITERIA
- Technical approach (30%)
- Experience and qualifications (25%)
- Project timeline (20%)
- Cost proposal (15%)
- Support and maintenance (10%)

6. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
- Technical proposal
- Financial proposal
- Company profile
- Team resumes
- Project portfolio
- References from previous clients

7. DEADLINE
Proposals must be submitted by December 31, 2024.
